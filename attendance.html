Method & caveats
  • Lateness = first clock-in vs each person's scheduled start in Factorial, 5-minute grace; approved-leave days and bank holidays excluded. Apparent lates over 4 hours are treated as schedule mismatches, not lateness.
  • Overtime = minutes clocked beyond that day's scheduled minutes, summed; shortfall summed separately. Includes unapproved/pending time, so Factorial's approved figure may differ slightly. Part-timers are measured against their own schedules.
  • Remaining holiday allowance isn't shown yet (not cleanly exposed by the API) — taken + booked only.
