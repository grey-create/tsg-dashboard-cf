💡 What it does well — and what it can't (yet)

✓ Works great

  • Rankings and trends across the last 12, 24, or 36 months — e.g. "top customers by 12m value", "monthly revenue this year"
  • Lifetime totals — "our biggest customer ever", "highest conversion rates", "customers who've spent £100k+"
  • Individual customer drilldowns — click any row to see their full timeline back to 2008, or ask the AI about a specific company by name
  • Quote vs order conversion analysis across any of the above

⚠ Doesn't work yet

  • Ranking customers within a specific past year — e.g. "best customer in 2012" won't return a list
  • Custom historical date ranges older than 3 years
All historical data from 2008 onwards is still in the system — click any customer to see their full timeline. The limitations above are about what the AI can rank across customers, not what's stored. We may add older-year ranking later.
